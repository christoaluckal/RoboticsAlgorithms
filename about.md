## About

CSE 4/568 course offers a gentle introduction to autonomy and robotics using the F1tenth platform. The course covers most aspects of robot autonomy such as perception, estimation, planning and control. Students work on programming assignments, in-class activites and quizes for an involved classroom experience. With a flip classroom structure, lectures are pre-recorded and classes are used for hands-on activities and discussions. All exams are eliminated and students are evaluated on practical understanding of the concepts taught in class.
